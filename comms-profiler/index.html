A quick note before you start

We know that people engage with information differently — and that's a good thing. Some of us prefer to dive into video and conversation. Others like time to read, reflect, and come back with questions. And others of us sit somewhere in between.

This short profiler helps us understand how you prefer to receive information, so we can make sure the communication we send you actually works for you.

✦ There are no right or wrong answers — every preference is equally valid.
✦ This isn't a test and it isn't an assessment of your capability or attitude.
✦ Your responses are used solely to tailor how we communicate with you — for no other purpose.
✦ It takes about 5 minutes.
✦ You'll see your result straight away.
